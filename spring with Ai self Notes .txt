## ЁЯМР Types of AI / LLM-based Models

### 1я╕ПтГг **Chat Models (Conversational)**

* Human jaisa baat karte hain.
* Based on **autoregressive (decoder-only)** models.
* Fine-tuned on dialogues, instructions, safety rules.
* тЬЕ Example: **ChatGPT, LLaMA-2-Chat, Mistral-Instruct, Vicuna, Claude**

---

### 2я╕ПтГг **Speech-to-Text Models (ASR тАУ Automatic Speech Recognition)**

* Voice ko text me convert karte hain.
* Audio input lete hain aur transcription output dete hain.
* тЬЕ Example: **Whisper (OpenAI), DeepSpeech (Mozilla), wav2vec 2.0 (Meta)**

---

### 3я╕ПтГг **Text-to-Speech Models (TTS)**

* Text ko natural sounding voice me bolte hain.
* Use: Virtual assistants, audiobooks, accessibility.
* тЬЕ Example: **VALL-E (Microsoft), Tacotron, Coqui TTS, ElevenLabs**

---

### 4я╕ПтГг **Multimodal Models**

* Ek hi model jo **text + image + audio + video** sab handle karta hai.
* Example: Image input + text output, ya text input + image output.
* тЬЕ Example: **GPT-4V (Vision), Gemini, LLaVA, Kosmos-1, CLIP**

---

### 5я╕ПтГг **Code Models**

* Programming help karne ke liye trained hote hain.
* тЬЕ Example: **Codex (GitHub Copilot), StarCoder, CodeLLaMA**

---

### 6я╕ПтГг **Domain-Specific Models**

* Special purpose models jo ek hi field ke liye optimized hote hain:

  * **Medical** тЖТ Med-PaLM
  * **Legal** тЖТ LawGPT

## ЁЯФе Hugging Face kya hai?

Socho **GitHub jaisa ek website/platform**, lekin **AI aur Machine Learning ke models** ke liye.

* Agar aapko **pre-trained model** chahiye (jaise ChatGPT jaisa chhota model, speech-to-text, translation, sentiment analysis), to Hugging Face par aapko **ready-made models** mil jaate hain.
* Wahan **datasets** bhi hote hain (Wikipedia text, movie reviews, images, audio, etc.) jinse aap apna model train kar sakte ho.
* Aur aap apne banaye models/datasets ko **upload karke share** bhi kar sakte ho.
